State Membership

Join HKM State Library as a State member and enjoy unlimited access to our comprehensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

About State Membership

The HKM State Library offers State membership to individuals who wish to have permanent access to our vast collection of resources. This membership provides you with lifelong privileges to utilize all library facilities and services.

State members enjoy uninterrupted access to our comprehensive collection of books, reference materials, research resources, digital archives, and specialized collections including Odisha Collection, Tribal Studies, Women Studies, and more.

Membership Benefits

  • Unlimited access to all library sections and collections
  • Borrowing privileges for books and periodicals
  • Access to Research and Reference Section
  • Use of reading rooms and study spaces
  • Access to digital resources and online databases
  • Priority reservation for library events and programs
  • Lifetime validity with no renewal required

This membership is ideal for researchers, scholars, students, and lifelong learners who require continuous access to library resources throughout their academic and professional journey.

Membership Pricing

Price Amount Status
Application Form ₹100/- Only Non-refundable
Security Deposit ₹25/- Only Refundable (After 3 Years)

Note: The security deposit of ₹25/- is refundable after completion of 3 years of membership. Application form fee is non-refundable.
