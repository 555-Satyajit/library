Library Profile

Santhakabi Bhima Bhoi Library (SKBB)

Santhakabi Bhima Bhoi Library is an integral part of the Odisha State Library system, serving as a knowledge and cultural resource center with a focus on literature, research, and public access to information.

About the Library

The Santhakabi Bhima Bhoi Library functions as a specialized library dedicated to preserving literary heritage while supporting modern learning and research needs. The library houses a wide range of books, periodicals, newspapers, and reference materials.

The library is committed to providing equitable access to knowledge, promoting reading habits, and supporting academic and cultural activities.

Santhakabi Bhima Bhoi Library – Reading Hall

Library Timings

Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Saturday: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Sunday & Government Holidays: Closed

Library Rules

Silence must be maintained inside the library premises. Borrowing privileges are subject to membership rules. Library materials must be handled with care.

Membership

Membership is open to students, researchers, and the general public as per prescribed norms and submission of valid identification.
