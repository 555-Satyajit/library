Building

The State Library is functioning in a four storied building with 70,000 sq.ft (350 ft breadth × 200 ft length) plinth area. The building has two mezzanine floors in the pattern of the National Library, Kolkata.

Timing

The Library functions in two shifts from 8 AM to 8 PM on all working days including Sundays. On Second Saturday it operates from 10 AM to 5 PM. Monday is weekly holiday.
