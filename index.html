About Us

Preserving Knowledge,
Promoting Cultural Heritage

The Harekrushna Mahatab Library is dedicated to the preservation, documentation, and dissemination of literary, historical, and cultural resources. Our mission is to make knowledge accessible to researchers, students, and the public through a modern digital-first approach.

By combining traditional archival values with contemporary technology, we ensure that Odisha’s intellectual and cultural heritage remains available for generations to come.

Learn More

Our Vision

To become a leading knowledge hub that bridges history, culture, and modern research through digital innovation.

Our Mission

To preserve rare documents, promote reading culture, and provide open access to verified archival resources.
