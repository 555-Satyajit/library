ORGANIZATION OF ODISHA STATE LIBRARY

From the organization point of view, the Library is divided into three Divisions, namely Administrative Services Division (ASD), Technical Services Division (TSD), and Reader's Services Division (RSD).

ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES DIVISION

ASD deals with the matters in connection with Administrative, Accounts, Finance, establishment, Garden, Canteen, Stores, Vehicle, Telephone, Cycle Stand, etc.

TECHNICAL SERVICES DIVISION

TSD deals with all technical activities such as Book Selection, Acquisition and Technical Processing i.e. classification, cataloguing etc. Apart from this, books received as gratis under Press and Registration of Books Act, 1867, from Raja Rammohan Roy Library Foundation and other sources are processed in this division.

READER SERVICES DIVISION

RSD deals with different services to users such as membership registration services, reading room services for general readers and research scholars in different sections, reprography services, reference and referral services, Bibliographic Services including library services for Children and visually impaired person.
